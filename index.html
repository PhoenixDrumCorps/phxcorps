Our Mission

Music.
Fun.
Friends.
Flexibility.

Phoenix, a Spectrum Performing Arts ensemble, our mission is to deliver complelling performances that captivate audiences while remaining accessible in both cost and time commitment. We are dedicated to making the marching arts an inclusive pursuit, welcoming individuals of all experience levels and backgrounds.

Our Purpose

Operate and maintain a competitive drum and bugle corps dedicated to educating its members in music, marching, and the visual performing arts, while also providing support and programming for the betterment of the public

Diversity Statement

Every member of Phoenix is recognized for their inherent worth and dignity. We are committed to fostering a culture of tolerance, sensitivity, understanding, and mutual respect, while supporting each individual in striving to reach their fullest potential.

Serve the Community

Phoenix is committed to extending its impact beyond the competitive arena by providing programs, resources, and community engagement initiatives that serve the public good. Through outreach, education, and performance opportunities, we strive to enrich the communities we serve and inspire a broader appreciation for the marching arts.

Diversity Statement

Every Voice
Belongs Here

Phoenix actively cultivates a culture of inclusion, mutual respect, and support. We believe that a diverse corps is a stronger corps.

  • Promoting tolerance, sensitivity, understanding, and mutual respect among all members.
  • Supporting each member in striving to reach their own unique potential.
